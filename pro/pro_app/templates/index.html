Customized Softwares

Customized software is software that is designed and developed for a specific organization or user, rather than for the mass market. It is created to meet the unique needs of a business or organization, and can be used to streamline operations and gain a competitive advantage.

Web Design & Development

Web design and development is an umbrella term that describes the process of creating a website. Like the name suggests, it involves two major skill sets: web design and web development. Web design determines the look and feel of a website, while web development determines how it functions.

Sotware Testing

Software testing is an important process in the software development lifecycle . It involves verifying and validating that a software application is free of bugs, meets the technical requirements set by its design and development , and satisfies user requirements efficiently and effectively.

E-Commerce Sites

E-commerce software is a stand-alone program or software suite that enables you to sell your products and services online. It allows you to streamline all of your processes from inventory to sales through the backend and makes it easy for a customer to buy from the front end, which is your website.

Hosting

Websites are stored – or “hosted” – on a publicly-accessible computer (a server). Some websites require an entire server to themselves. Others can share a server with 100s of other websites. The storage space, and the features that come with it, make up your hosting plan.
